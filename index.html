About Sheohar Nagar Parishad

Sheohar Nagar Parishad is committed to providing transparent, efficient, and citizen-friendly urban governance to the residents of Sheohar, Bihar.

Our mission is to develop Sheohar as a smart, clean, and sustainable city with modern civic amenities. We work towards improving infrastructure, sanitation, public health, and overall quality of life for our citizens.

The Nagar Parishad administers the municipal functions across all wards of Sheohar, ensuring proper delivery of services including water supply, solid waste management, street lighting, drainage, and urban planning.
