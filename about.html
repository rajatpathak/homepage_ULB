About Sheohar Nagar Parishad

Committed to Smart, Transparent & Citizen-Friendly Urban Governance

Our Mission & Vision

Sheohar Nagar Parishad is committed to providing transparent, efficient, and citizen-friendly urban governance to the residents of Sheohar, Bihar.

Our mission is to develop Sheohar as a smart, clean, and sustainable city with modern civic amenities. We strive to enhance the quality of life for our citizens by implementing innovative solutions, maintaining high standards of public services, and promoting participatory governance.

Our Objectives

  • Ensure efficient delivery of essential municipal services
  • Maintain and develop urban infrastructure
  • Promote cleanliness and environmental sustainability
  • Implement digital governance for transparency
  • Engage citizens in development initiatives
  • Ensure equitable distribution of resources
