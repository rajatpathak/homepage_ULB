About Sheohar Nagar Parishad

Sheohar Nagar Parishad, established in 1956, has been at the forefront of providing efficient municipal services to the citizens of Sheohar. With a population of over 45,000 residents spread across 26 wards, we are committed to transforming Sheohar into a model smart city through transparent governance, modern infrastructure, and citizen-centric services.

Our Vision

To create a clean, green, and digitally empowered urban center that provides world-class civic amenities and ensures quality of life for all citizens through transparent and accountable governance.

Our Mission

To deliver efficient, responsive, and citizen-friendly municipal services through innovative technology solutions, sustainable development practices, and active citizen participation.

Our Leadership

Mayor

Shri Rajesh Kumar

"My commitment is to serve the citizens of Sheohar with complete transparency, efficiency, and dedication. We are working tirelessly to transform Sheohar into a smart and sustainable city that our future generations will be proud of."

Shri Rajesh Kumar brings over 15 years of public service experience and has been instrumental in implementing digital governance initiatives across the municipality.

Executive Officer

Smt. Priya Sharma, IAS

"We are implementing cutting-edge digital governance solutions to ensure seamless service delivery and meaningful citizen engagement in all municipal operations. Our focus is on making every service accessible, transparent, and efficient."

Smt. Priya Sharma is a 2015-batch IAS officer who has successfully led multiple e-governance projects and municipal reform initiatives across Bihar.
