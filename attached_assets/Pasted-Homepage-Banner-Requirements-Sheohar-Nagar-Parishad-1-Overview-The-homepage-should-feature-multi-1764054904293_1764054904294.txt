Homepage Banner Requirements — Sheohar Nagar Parishad
1. Overview

The homepage should feature multiple high-quality AI-generated banners that visually represent Sheohar, Bihar in a realistic and culturally accurate manner. These banners will enhance the visual appeal and reflect the identity, development, and governance initiatives of the Nagar Parishad.

2. Number of Banners

A total of 5 (five) banners must be created and integrated into the homepage slider.

3. Banner Specifications

Dimensions: 1920 × 720 px (full-width responsive banner)

Style: Realistic, clean, modern, and suitable for a government website

Tone: Professional, civic-oriented, citizen-friendly

Color Palette: Natural tones, greens, blues, soft daylight ambience

Format: High-resolution PNG/JPEG

Usage: Rotating slider with smooth transitions

4. Banner Themes (AI-Generated)
Banner 1: Sheohar City Overview

Clean road view of Sheohar

Typical Bihar small-town architecture

Greenery, open sky, soft sunlight

Banner 2: Nagar Parishad Development Work

Roads, streetlights, water supply pipelines, drainage work

Municipal workers in action (generic, non-identifiable)

“Urban Development” theme

Banner 3: Clean & Green Sheohar

Parks, plants, tree plantation

Clean streets

Environmental-friendly city theme

Banner 4: Citizen Services & Digital Governance

Illustrations/realistic visuals of smart governance

Online services, digital payments, transparency

Government buildings in the background

Banner 5: Cultural & Community Spirit of Sheohar

Local market street

Cultural moments, people, community interaction

Representing daily life of Sheohar

5. Integration Requirements

Implement banners in a homepage slider

Auto-slide with configurable interval (default: 5 seconds)

Manual left/right navigation arrows

Mobile responsive scaling

Text overlay support (Title, Subtitle, CTA Buttons)

6. Text Overlay (Optional for Each Banner)

Main Title: “Sheohar Nagar Parishad”

Subtext: “Committed to Smart, Transparent & Citizen-Friendly Urban Governance”
